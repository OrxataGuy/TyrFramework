# Community Distributions

Tyr follows a hybrid open community model. The main repository is maintained by [@orxataguy](https://github.com/orxataguy) and published to npm as [`@orxataguy/tyr`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@orxataguy/tyr).

Anyone is free to fork the project, make improvements, and publish their own distribution under a personal or organization npm scope — no permission required. Each fork is an independent package owned and maintained by its author.

## Adding Your Fork

To have your distribution listed here:

1. Open a pull request against the [main repository](https://github.com/orxataguy/TyrFramework)
2. Add a row to the table above with your fork's details
3. Your fork must be publicly available on both GitHub and npm

Please keep the description to one sentence — what makes your fork different or what problem it solves.
